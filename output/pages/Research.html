Research

My current research is in post-quantum cryptography. In particular I am interested in new post-quantum primitives; realizing classical cryptosystems over post-quantum primitives; protocols; efficient implementations; and quantum cryptanalysis. I am primarily focused on isogeny-based schemes, built on the ideas underlying SIKE and CSIDH. In the past, I have worked on combinatorial design theory, particularly on configuration orderings of balanced incomplete block designs.
